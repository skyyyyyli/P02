Brand Ideas

Neito is a Japanese design furniture and decoration company in the Vancouver, dedicated to creating a comfortable and simply living environment. We offer our clients popular works from leading Japanese designers; and exclusive insights into the most relevant products and brands in Canada today.

By showing examples of the most innovative lifestyles observed in various furniture product designs and interior design - Neito aims to provide a more innovative, Japanese and Canadian style of products that make people's rooms more lively and comfortable.

Art Direction

We intend to use minimalist and sophisticated typography to ensure a smooth overall effect while matching our theme product design. White and grey elements will be interspersed in the layout of the overall design. The fonts we choose are Optima & Din Pro. All these two fonts are sans-serif font and then conform to the spirit of minimalist and comfortable.
