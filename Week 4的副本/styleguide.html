Art direction & Fonts

We intend to use minimalist and sophisticated typography to ensure a smooth overall effect while matching our theme energy pumping. Yellow and some beverage elements will be interspersed in the layout of the overall design. The fonts we choose are Helvetica & Futura. All these two fonts are sans-serif font and then conform to the spirit of minimalist but full of energy.

Our goal is to present refreshing but simply designs through clean layouts and different beverage photos. Thus we choose a low saturation yellow and green to use from shallow to deep. Colors with low saturation can visually give people a more layered feel and are easier to match with other elements. This is also in line with the purpose of our company.
