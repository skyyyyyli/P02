    Art direction & Fonts

    We intend to use minimalist and sophisticated typography to ensure a smooth overall effect while matching our theme product design. White and grey elements will be interspersed in the layout of the overall design. The fonts we choose are Optima & Din Pro. All these two fonts are sans-serif font and then conform to the spirit of minimalist and comfortable.

    Our goal is to present elaborate but simple designs through clean layouts and various interior design pattern. Thus we choose a low saturation of white and black to use from shallow to deep. Colors with low saturation can visually give people a more layered feel and are easier to match with other elements. This is also in line with the purpose of our company.
